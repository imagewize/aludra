Surface rhythm

One step lighter than the band.

Two card sections run back to back on the service page. They stay legible as separate sections not by adding a third surface colour, but by swapping figure and ground: the band a card sits on decides how light the card is. Each band publishes --aludra-band-card; the cards read that token and never name a colour of their own.

white#FFFFFF Card on a base band
base#FAF7F2 Page surface · card on a tertiary band
tertiary#F0E9DD Tinted band
border-light#E5DED2 Hairline between bands
